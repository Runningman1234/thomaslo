Backstory.

I was always passionate about music and learned piano and trumpet at a early age. In 2010, I found like-minded friends and together, we coordinated gala concerts to raise funds for the Markham Stouffville Hospital Foudation. The following year I decided to name our organization Helping with Harmony. To date, we have fundraised over $6000.
